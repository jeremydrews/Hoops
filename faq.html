FAQ

Q : How to buy a product ?
A : Press the product you want to buy, a form will appear and fill it out.
Q : What if my product that i bought hasn't arrived and there is no news about that ?
A : The first possibility is that the product has been received but we haven't updated it yet. Usually we update the same day or one after the product is received.
Q : What if the item received is damaged ?
A : You can come to our nearest store with a shopping receipt and the damage also has terms and condition.
Q : What if we have paid for the product and want to change the product ?
A : You cannot change the product if you have confirmed and paid for the product.
